Projects & Research

Multimodal Contrastive Learning for Cancer Cell Identification Spring 2026

Developed a multimodal contrastive learning framework as an alternative approach to differential expression analysis, integrating transcriptomic features with representation learning to identify early malignant transition.

Accepted — NeurIPS 2026 Workshop on Multimodal Foundation Models for Life Sciences

Risk Communication Analysis of Drug Labels using Natural Language Inference Summer 2026

Built a framework for longitudinal analysis of risk communication in drug labels using natural language inference and zero-shot text classification, presenting the pipeline and findings in a paper under submission to LREC 2026.

Under submission — LREC 2026

Measuring Information Safety in LLMs Fall 2025

Designed a benchmarking framework to measure political bias and influence in large language models, with feature design drawn from computational social science. Built backend infrastructure for LLM-Arena-style LLM-versus-LLM evaluation using Supabase.
